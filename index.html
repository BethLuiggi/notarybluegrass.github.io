Policies & Important Notes

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 5 hours before the scheduled appointment will be charged the full signing fee.

No-Show / Refusal to Sign

Client no-shows and refusals to sign at the table are subject to the full signing fee.

Wait Time Policy

We allow a 15-minute grace period with no contact before departing. If you need us to wait, please contact us. A $15 fee applies at the 15-minute mark, plus $1 per minute after.

Re-Draw Signings

Re-draws required due to lender error (not notary error) are billed at the standard applicable signing rate.
